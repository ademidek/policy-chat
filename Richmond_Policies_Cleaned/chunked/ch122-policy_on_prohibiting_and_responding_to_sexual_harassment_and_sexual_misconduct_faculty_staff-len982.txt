2. pre-hearing submission deadlines.
a. submission of witness lists. the respondent and complainant shall provide the hearing officer or
their designee their respective lists of witnesses by 5:00 p.m. on the seventh (7) business day before
the hearing.
b. submission of documentary, electronic, and other evidence. the respondent and complainant
shall submit copies of all documentary, electronic, or other evidence to the hearing officer or their
designee by 5:00 p.m. on the fourth (4th) business day before the hearing.
c. statement of objections to the title ix investigative report. the complainant and respondent shall
provide the hearing board with their respective objections to the final title ix investigative report, if
any, by 5:00 pm on the fourth (4th) business day before the hearing.
