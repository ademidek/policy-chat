application: software designed to address various needs, ranging from simple utilities and productivity tools
to complex enterprise system solutions. typically, they have a user interface that allows people to digitally
interact with them, and they may also rely on data processing, storage and communication functionality to

authentication: authentication is the process by which an electronic system or software application confirms
that a person or device really is who or what it is claiming to be and through which access to the requested
resource is authorized. strong authentication protocols help both to protect personal and university
information and prevent misuse of university resources.
