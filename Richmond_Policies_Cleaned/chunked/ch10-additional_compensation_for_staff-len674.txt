secondary jobs
an employee may be compensated for work unrelated to and distinctly separate from their primary job.
secondary jobs can be utilized for employees working two part-time jobs or one full-time job and a part-time
job. secondary jobs can be regular staff positions, temporary positions, or on-call positions
