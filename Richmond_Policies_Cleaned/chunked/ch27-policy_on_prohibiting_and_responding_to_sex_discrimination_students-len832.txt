2. purpose / no unreasonable burden. supportive measures shall be designed to: (i) protect the safety
of a complainant, respondent, witness, or the campus community; or (ii) provide support to a
complainant, respondent, or witness during an investigation, formal resolution process, or an
alternative resolution process. supportive measures are not used for punitive or disciplinary reasons.
supportive measures should not unreasonably burden either the complainant or the respondent.
