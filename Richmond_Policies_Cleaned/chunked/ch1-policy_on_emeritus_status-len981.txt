university of richmond
policy manual
purpose:
this policy provides clarity as to the benefits associated with emeritus status.
scope:
this policy applies to the university of richmond and all of its affiliates. as used in this policy, the term
"affiliates" means organizations or entities in which the university owns a controlling interest or has the right
to elect the majority of the entity's governing board.

policy statement:
hrm-1006.1 – definitions
retirement: a person is considered to be retired or a retiree for purposes of this policy if they terminate
employment with the university after meeting all of the university's eligibility requirements for retirement.
hrm-1006.2 – policy statement
as outlined in the faculty handbook, certain faculty who retire from the university after providing long and
exceptional service to the university may be eligible for emeritus status
