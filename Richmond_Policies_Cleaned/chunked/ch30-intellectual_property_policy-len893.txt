f. entirely online courses
1. "entirely online course" is a course delivered to students entirely via a computer
network. an entirely online course may comprise course materials including without
limitation lecture notes, course outlines, reading lists, handouts, exercises and
examination questions and answers. an entirely online course may also incorporate
computer programs.
