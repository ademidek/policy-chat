university of richmond
policy manual
purpose:
this policy is designed to ensure that the university of richmond complies with applicable laws and
regulations regarding political campaign activity.
scope:
this policy applies to the use of university resources and facilities by students, faculty, and staff and to the
conduct of university employees acting in an official capacity. it also applies to organizations or entities in
which the university owns a controlling interest or has the right to elect the majority of the entity's governing
board (hereinafter "affiliates").
this policy does not apply to or restrict in any way the discussion of political issues, teaching of politics or
campaign-related subjects, or academic research involving political issues, or campaigns
