3. authorization
the management and maintenance of authorizations is a shared responsibility of information services
and local application administrators and data stewards. all authorizations must be granted in accordance
with the concept of least privilege. only authorizations that are necessary to perform job tasks should be
granted. all others should be denied/disabled.
4. provisioning
a. birthright entitlements: when an account is created in the university's central identity system,
certain authorizations are immediately created with it, such as the ability to authenticate against
ur's enterprise authentication systems, access to ur's data network, and several online
resources
