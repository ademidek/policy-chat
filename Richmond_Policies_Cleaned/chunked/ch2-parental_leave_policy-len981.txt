policy statement:
as outlined in this policy, the university of richmond provides parental leave to employees to care for and
bond with a newborn, newly adopted, or newly placed foster child within 12 months of the birth, adoption, or
state placement of a child; or when required to fulfill the legal requirements for an adoption.
parental leave does not apply to situations in which there has been a change in status of legal custody that is not
also accompanied by an adoption, birth, or foster placement.
