the university of richmond prohibits discrimination against applicants, students, faculty, or staff on the
basis of race, color, religion, national or ethnic origin, ethnicity, age, sex, gender, sexual orientation, gender
identity, gender expression, disability, military or veteran status, or any classification protected by local, state,
or federal law.
1
this prohibition includes discrimination based on protected status (as defined in this policy). harassment
that creates a hostile environment (as defined in this policy) is a form of discrimination that is also
prohibited.
this policy applies to discrimination alleged to occur in any education program or activity operated by the
university in the united states. this prohibition includes discrimination in the admission or retention of
students based on protected status and employment
