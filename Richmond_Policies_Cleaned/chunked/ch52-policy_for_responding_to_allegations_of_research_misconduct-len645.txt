. time for completion
the investigation is to be completed within 120 days of beginning it, including conducting the investigation,
preparing the report of findings, providing the draft report for comment and sending the final report to ori.
however, if the rio determines that the investigation will not be completed within this 120-day period,
he/she will submit to ori a written request for an extension, setting forth the reasons for the delay. the
rio will ensure that periodic progress reports are filed with ori, if ori grants the request for an extension
and directs the filing of such reports.
