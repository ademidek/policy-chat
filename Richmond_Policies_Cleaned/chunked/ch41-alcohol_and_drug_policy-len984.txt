stu-1002 – alcohol, tobacco and other drug policy
o distribution of more than one ounce but less than 5 pounds is a class 5 felony punishable
with up to 5 years in prison and a fine up to $2500.
o distribution of more than 5 pounds of marijuana is punishable with 5-30 years in prison.
o distribution of 100 kg or more of a mixture or substance containing marijuana: felony 20
years – life and $1,000,000 fine
• manufacture of marijuana: felony 5-30 years and a fine not to exceed $10,000.
 other drugs
• possession
o schedule i or ii drugs – felony- imprisonment from 1 to 10 years or jail for
up to 12 months and a fine of up to $2,500.
o schedule iii drugs – misdemeanor – jail for up to 12 months and a fine up to $2500
o schedule iv drugs – misdemeanor – jail for up to 6 months and a fine up to $1000
o schedule v drugs – misdemeanor – fine up to $500
o schedule vi drugs – misdemeanor – fine up to $250
• sales/distribution – schedule i or ii drugs
