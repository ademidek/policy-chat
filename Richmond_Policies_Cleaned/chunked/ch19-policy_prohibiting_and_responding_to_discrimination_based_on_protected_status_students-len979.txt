gov-3102.4 – the role of the title ix coordinators
the university's title ix coordinators are responsible for ensuring the university's compliance with this
policy and applicable laws prohibiting discrimination. this policy sets forth the obligations of the
appropriate title ix coordinator upon receipt of information that may reasonably constitute discrimination
or a complaint.
