. privacy. the title ix coordinator, the investigator, the appellate officer and others involved in the
investigation, formal resolution process, and alternative resolution process will take reasonable steps to
protect the privacy of the complainant, the respondent and any witnesses during the process. the
commitment to privacy, however, shall not be implemented in a manner that restricts the ability of the
complainant and respondent to obtain and present evidence, including by speaking to witnesses,
consulting with family members, confidential resources, or advisors, or otherwise preparing for an
investigation, formal resolution process, or alternative resolution process.
