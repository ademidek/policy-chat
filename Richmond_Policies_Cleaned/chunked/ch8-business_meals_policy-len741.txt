meals with visitors and guests: the university allows payment for reasonable and necessary meal expenses
incurred during a meal at which faculty, staff, or students and external parties are present for the purpose of
discussing university business. examples of such meals include meals with job candidates and meals and
receptions while hosting visiting scholars.
employee-only meals: university payment for business meals attended only by ur employees which do not
involve travel is only permitted if documentation supporting the meal clearly establishes the business purpose
of the meal and that the business could not reasonably take place any other time or manner
