. general
the rio is responsible for:
• promptly taking all reasonable and practical steps to obtain custody of all research records and evidence
needed to conduct the research misconduct proceeding, inventory the records and evidence, and
sequester them in a secure manner.
• taking all reasonable and practical steps to ensure the cooperation of respondents and other university
members with research misconduct proceedings, including, but not limited to their providing
information, research records and evidence.
• providing confidentiality to those involved in the research misconduct proceeding as required by 42
cfr § 93.108, other applicable law, and university policy.
• determining whether each person involved in handling an allegation of research misconduct has an
unresolved personal, professional or :financial conflict of interest and taking appropriate action,
including recusal, to ensure that no person with such a conflict is involved in the research misconduct
proceeding.
