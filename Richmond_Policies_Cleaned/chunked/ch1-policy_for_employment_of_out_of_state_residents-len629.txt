university of richmond
policy manual
purpose:
the purpose of this policy is to establish the framework for hiring or reassigning university of richmond
employees to perform work outside the commonwealth of virginia, and to ensure the university's
compliance with state and local tax law, workers compensation and similar insurance requirements, and other
relevant laws.
scope:
this policy applies to faculty, staff and student employees of the university of richmond and its affiliates
(collectively, the university) who work and reside outside of the commonwealth of virginia, but in the
united states
