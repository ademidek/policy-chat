. notice and reporting to ori and cooperation with ori
the rio has lead responsibility for ensuring that the university:
• files an annual report with ori containing the information prescribed by ori.
• sends to ori with the annual report such other aggregated information as ori may prescribe on the
university's research misconduct proceedings and the university's compliance with 42 cfr part 93.
• notifies ori immediately if, at any time during the research misconduct proceeding, it has reason to
believe that health or safety of the public is at risk, federal grant resources or interests are threatened,
research activities should be suspended, there is reasonable indication of possible violations of civil or
criminal law, federal action is required to protect the interests of those involved in the research
misconduct proceeding, the university believes that the research misconduct proceeding may be made
public prematurely, or the research community or the public should be informed.
