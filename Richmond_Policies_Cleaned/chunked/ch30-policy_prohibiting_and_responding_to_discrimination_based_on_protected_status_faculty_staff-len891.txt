2. process for appeal.
i. the complainant or the respondent may file an appeal with the director of compliance, or
their designee, (the "appellate officer") within five (5) business days of being notified of
the title ix coordinator's decision to dismiss the complaint.
ii. the appeal must be in writing and must be emailed to the appellate officer with a copy to
the title ix coordinator
