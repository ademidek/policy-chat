prm-4001.7 – events with beer trucks
events with beer trucks must comply with all the procedures listed above. in addition, the event manager
must obtain an abc license for the event. the abc license must be obtained by a university staff or
faculty member. students cannot obtain the abc license. when applying for the license, the university
employee should state their name and that the license is on behalf of the university. for example, jane
doe on behalf of the university of richmond.
the abc license must be requested and paid for on-line: https://www.abc.virginia.gov/licenses/get-a-
license
