3. notice to complainant and respondent. the title ix coordinator shall promptly notify the
complainant, in writing, of the dismissal of a complaint and the basis for such dismissal. if the
dismissal occurs after the respondent has been notified of the complaint, the title ix coordinator
shall notify the respondent, in writing, of the dismissal of a complaint and the basis for such
dismissal at the same time that the complainant is notified. such notice shall include the rights of
the complainant and respondent to file an appeal of the dismissal and the basis for such appeal as
set forth in this policy.
