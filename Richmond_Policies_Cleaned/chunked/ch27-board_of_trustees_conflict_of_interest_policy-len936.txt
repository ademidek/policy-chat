sanctions
violation of this conflict of interest policy may result in disciplinary action, which for employees can
include oral reprimand, written reprimand, probation, suspension or immediate termination of employment,
and for others, such action as may be necessary in the opinion of the board of trustees to protect the
interests and comply with the responsibilities and obligations of the university.
