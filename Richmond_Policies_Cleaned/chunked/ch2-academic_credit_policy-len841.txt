acd-1001.1 – policy
academic credit is awarded to a student upon the successful completion of an approved instructional course,
or by the demonstration of competency, demonstration of proficiency, or fulfillment of learning objectives.
the university of richmond supports a variety of academic credit systems, each approved and maintained by
the faculty of the respective school(s). each school is responsible for demonstrating that these requirements
are met for all courses.
