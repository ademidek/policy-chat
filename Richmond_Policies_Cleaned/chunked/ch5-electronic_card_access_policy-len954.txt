. students living off campus do not have access to the residence halls, but access may be
granted on an as needed basis with the approval of the richmond college or westhampton college
deans' offices.
student access to the residence halls is based on the housing contract dates. all access privileges are
removed and the student's campus card account is inactivated when one card services receives a change
of status due to withdrawal, transfer, or suspension. residence hall access and visitation privileges are
removed when students move off campus. access privileges may be revoked due to misconduct.
students may be granted access to certain buildings and labs based on their status within the
department/building (i.e. majors and research students, students in the arts needing access to practice
rooms and art spaces, etc.).
faculty and staff access privileges:
administrative buildings are generally unlocked during business and/or classroom hours
