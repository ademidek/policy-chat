. closing statements. prior to the initial deliberations by the hearing board, the respondent and

f. hearing board deliberations. the hearing officer will excuse all parties so that the members of the
hearing board may deliberate in private on the issue of responsibility for the charges at issue.
g. initial notification to the parties. after deliberations on the issue of responsibility are completed, the
hearing officer shall meet separately with the respondent and complainant to notify them of the
decision of the hearing board on the issue of responsibility.
