4. notice of meetings. the title ix coordinator or the investigator shall provide the complainant,
respondent, and any witness with written notice of the date, time, location (if not virtual), participants
and purpose of each meeting with the investigator. such notice shall be provided with sufficient time for
the complainant, respondent, or witness to prepare to participate in the meeting.
5. advisors/other persons. the complainant and the respondent may be accompanied by the advisor of
their choice during all meetings with the investigator. the advisor may, but is not required to be, an
attorney. neither the complainant or respondent may have a person other than their advisor present
during meetings with the investigator.
