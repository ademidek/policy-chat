sec-2005.2 – policy
student access privileges:
resident students are automatically assigned 24/7 access to their residence hall and visitation to other
halls (except atlantic and pacific houses) from 8 a.m. – 2 a.m. monday – sunday (except on home
football game saturdays). visitation to atlantic and pacific houses is 8 a.m. – 2 a.m. monday – thursday
and restricted access from friday through the weekend.
residents of university forest apartments and gateway village also have visitation privileges to the
residence halls
