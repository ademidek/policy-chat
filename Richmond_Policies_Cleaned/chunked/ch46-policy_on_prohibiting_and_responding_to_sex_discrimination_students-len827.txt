. access and response to the investigative report and evidence.
a. access to preliminary report. the title ix coordinator shall provide both the complainant and
the respondent with access to the preliminary investigative report and its appendices containing
relevant and permissible information. if the complaint is directed at a university program,
policy, or practice and not a specific respondent, the title ix coordinator shall provide the
appropriate university official(s) with access to the preliminary investigative report.
b. response to investigative report. the complainant and respondent will have ten (10) days
from the date that access was provided to submit a written response to the investigative report
and evidence in the appendices which the investigator will consider prior to completion of the
final report
