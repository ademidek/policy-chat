acd-8101.14 – deaccession
it is the intent of the museums to acquire objects with care so that it is seldom necessary to engage in
deaccession. nonetheless, the museums are aware that periodic evaluation of the collections and careful use
of deaccessioning may strengthen the quality of the museums' collection over time. as part of the museums'
periodic inventory process, items will be considered for deaccession (see section acd-8101.8,
documentation).

acd-8101 – museum collections management policy
