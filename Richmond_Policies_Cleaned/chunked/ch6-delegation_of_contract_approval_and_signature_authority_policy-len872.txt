fin-4409.2 – overview
the policy delegates approval and signature authority for university contracts. all university contracts must
be approved and signed in a manner consistent with this policy. individuals to whom contract approval and
signature authority is delegated under this policy may not delegate such authority to others. this policy must
be read in conjunction with the policies listed in the related policies and procedures section of this document.
