university of richmond
policy manual
scope:
this policy applies to the university of richmond employees and all of its affiliates. as used in this
policy, the term "employees" refers to staff and faculty; "affiliates" means organizations or entities in
which the university owns a controlling interest or has the right to elect the majority of the entity's
governing board.
