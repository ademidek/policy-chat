university of richmond
policy manual
purpose:
all members of the university community and third-party users of university facilities are responsible for
knowing and acting in accordance with applicable laws and university policies concerning alcohol when
hosting an event on campus. the purpose of this policy is to inform the university community and third-party
users of university facilities of the procedures and regulations they must comply with when hosting an event
with alcohol at the university.
scope:
these procedures apply to all members of the university community and third-party users of university
facilities that wish to host events with alcohol on university of richmond property.
all alcoholic beverages to be consumed at university approved events must be supplied by university
catering or in conjunction with a university approved caterer with an abc license. spider brews and
approved events with beer trucks are the only exceptions
