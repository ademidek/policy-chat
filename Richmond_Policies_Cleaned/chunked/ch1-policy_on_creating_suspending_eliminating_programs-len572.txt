university of richmond
policy manual
purpose:
the purpose of this policy is to ensure that all proposed changes to academic programs are thoroughly
reviewed by the faculty and academic leadership, and that the review and approval processes support
compliance with federal and sacscoc requirements. this document codifies the process and
documentation needed for approving new academic
programs, or modifying, suspending or eliminating
existing academic programs.
scope:
this policy applies to the university of richmond and all of its affiliates
