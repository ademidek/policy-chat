c. university decision and notification
1. decision by deciding official
the rio will transmit the final inquiry report and any comments to the do, who will determine in
writing whether an investigation is warranted. the inquiry is completed when the do makes this
determination.
2. notification to ori
within 30 calendar days of the do's decision that an investigation is warranted, the rio will provide
ori with the do' s written decision and a copy of the inquiry report
