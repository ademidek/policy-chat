gov-3108.4 – the role of the title ix coordinators
the university's title ix coordinators are responsible for ensuring the university's compliance with this
policy and title ix. this policy sets forth the obligations of the appropriate title ix coordinator upon
receipt of information that may reasonably constitute sex discrimination or a complaint.
for more information on the role of title ix coordinators at the university, see the university's
title
ix coordinators policy.
