the granting of emeritus status to employees by the board of trustees provides the holder the right to use the
title bestowed. emeritus status does not grant the holder any other university benefits. retiree benefits are not
linked to emeritus status and are governed by the university's retirement policies.
in rare instances, emeritus status may be granted by the board of trustees to an employee who does not meet
the criteria for retirement from the university
