. assessment and coordination of remedies by title ix coordinator
1. complaints against the university. if a complaint is directed at a university program, policy, or
practice and not a specific respondent, and the complainant and appropriate university officials have
not elected to pursue alternative resolution of the complaint, as described in section 3102.6(d) of this
policy, the title ix coordinator shall take the actions described below.
a. determination by title ix coordinator.
i. after reviewing the final investigative report and the parties' responses to the preliminary
investigative report, the title ix coordinator shall determine, based on a preponderance of the
evidence, whether the alleged discrimination occurred.
ii. the title ix coordinator shall consider only evidence and testimony that is relevant and not
impermissible, consistent with this policy.
