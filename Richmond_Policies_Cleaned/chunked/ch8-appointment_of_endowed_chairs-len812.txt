responsibilities of an endowed faculty chair
the holder of an endowed faculty chair is responsible for:
1. fulfilling the conditions of the gift and any specific duties identified at the time of appointment;
2. continuing to demonstrate evidence of excellence in teaching and scholarship, (research, or artistic
production as appropriate), as documented in annual reports submitted by the chair holder;
3. complying with the terms of the use of professional development funds, as specified in university
policy on the use of income from endowed chairs;
4. actively participating in the chair holder's home department and/or school; and
