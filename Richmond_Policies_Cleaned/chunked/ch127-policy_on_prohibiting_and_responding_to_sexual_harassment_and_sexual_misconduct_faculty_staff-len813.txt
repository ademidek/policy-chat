. the hearing record.
a. the hearing officer or their designee shall create a hearing record comprising the following:
i. the investigative report with its attachments; and
ii. the documentary, electronic, and other evidence submitted by the hearing board, the
complainant (if applicable), and the respondent (if applicable).
b. the hearing officer or their designee shall provide the hearing board, the complainant, and the
respondent with access to the hearing record two (2) business days prior to the hearing.
i. the hearing officer or their designee shall ensure that there are sufficient copies of the hearing
record available during the hearing for all hearing board members, the respondent and their
advisor, the complainant and their advisor and for testifying witnesses to use during their
testimony.
