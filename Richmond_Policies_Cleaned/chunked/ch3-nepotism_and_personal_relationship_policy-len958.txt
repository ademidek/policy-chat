hrm-4001 – employment of relatives
university of richmond | 2
intimate partner
a person with whom an employ is having or, within the last twelve months, had a romantic and/or sexual
relationship.
hr
m-4001.2 – policy
no supervisor may influence, directly or indirectly, the hiring, salary, promotion, performance appraisals,
work assignments or other working conditions of a family/household member or intimate partner.
i
f a situation emerges in which an employee may be in the position of supervising, directly or indirectly, a
family/household member or intimate partner as defined in this policy, the employee must discuss the
matter, confidentially, with their supervisor or with human resources to address the conflict and make
arrangements to ensure that employment-related decisions are made by someone who does not have such
a relationship.
