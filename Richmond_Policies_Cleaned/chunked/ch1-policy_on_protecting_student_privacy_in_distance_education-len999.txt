university of richmond
policy manual
purpose:
the university of richmond is committed to protecting student privacy for students enrolled in distance
education courses.
scope:
this policy applies to the education records of all current and former students of the university of richmond.
faculty, staff and students of the university are expected to comply with this policy.

acd-2005.1 – definitions
student
any person who attends or has attended the university of richmond whether physically present on a campus
or online and regarding whom the university maintains education records.
