scope:
this policy applies to the university of richmond and all of its affiliates. as used in this policy, the term
"affiliates" means organizations or entities in which the university owns a controlling interest or has the right
to elect the majority of the entity's governing board.

policy statement:
irm-3001.1 – purpose
the university of richmond provides network connectivity to faculty, staff, students, and visitors to facilitate
the educational mission of the institution. network connectivity can take the form of a wired connection, wi-
fi connection, or remote access connection via the campus vpn.
devices that connect to the campus network must satisfy minimum network security requirements to protect
the integrity of shared data, systems, and operations
