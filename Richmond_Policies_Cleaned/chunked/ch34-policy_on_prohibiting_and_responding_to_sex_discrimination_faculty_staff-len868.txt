4. supportive measures and steps to prevent discrimination and eliminate hostile environment if the
title ix coordinator dismisses a complaint, the title ix coordinator shall:
a. offer appropriate supportive measures to the complainant;
b. offer appropriate supportive measures to the respondent if the dismissal occurs after the
respondent has been notified of the complaint; and
c. take other prompt and effective steps reasonably calculated to end any sex discrimination,
eliminate any hostile environment or its effects, and prevent its recurrence.
5. appeal of decision to dismiss complaint. a complainant has a right to appeal the dismissal of a
complaint on the grounds set forth in this policy
