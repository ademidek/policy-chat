b. notice of determination/remedies
i. the title ix coordinator will provide the complainant and the appropriate university
official(s) with written notice of the title ix coordinator's determination as to whether sex
discrimination occurred. that notice shall include the rationale for such decision and the right

this policy.

in writing, of any remedies that will be implemented to restore or continue complainant's equal
