school of professional and continuing studies awards credit for prior learning. students can seek

aded 200u, experiential learning and portfolio preparation, then enroll in aded 201u,
portfolio submission and assessment. portfolios are reviewed by qualified faculty members who
recommend credit based on the extent and depth of each student's learning.
