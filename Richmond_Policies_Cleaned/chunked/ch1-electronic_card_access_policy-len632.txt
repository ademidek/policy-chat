university of richmond
policy manual
purpose:
this policy is designed to ensure card access to various doors on campus are dependent upon need and
appropriately maintained within the cbord campus card system.
scope:
this policy applies to all university of richmond faculty, staff, students, vendors and contractors. access is
based on the requirements that an individual department or building sets. while the university utilizes a
centralized mechanism for the provisioning and de-provisioning of access, each department within the
university still maintains access authority over their designated areas
