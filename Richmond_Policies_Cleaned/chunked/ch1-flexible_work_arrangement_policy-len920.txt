purpose:
the purpose of this policy is to describe the types of flexible work arrangements that may be available to
university staff members, the process for evaluating a request for a flexible work arrangement and the
approval process for such a request.
scope:
this policy applies to flexible work arrangements for certain exempt and non-exempt staff that are expected
to remain in place for 4 to 12 months. these arrangements should be reviewed annually. it is not intended to
apply to short-term, ad hoc arrangements to meet unexpected needs
