the university of richmond does not conduct random drug or alcohol testing, but does reserve
the right to test for drugs and/or alcohol for cause. should the university have a reasonable
suspicion that an employee is under the influence of illegal drugs, alcohol or marijuana or if the
employee is in an accident causing damage to university property, the employee may be required
to submit to drug/alcohol testing and/or mandatory referral into a substance misuse assistance or
rehabilitation program. refusal by an employee to comply with the applicable requirements may
be grounds for immediate dismissal from employment.
