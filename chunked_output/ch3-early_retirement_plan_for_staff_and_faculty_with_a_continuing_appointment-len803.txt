policy statement:
hrm-1008.1 – definitions
a) "effective date" means the date that the eligible employee's early retirement commences. an employee's effective date shall be specified in such employee's election form that has
been signed by the employee and approved by human resources. in general, the effective
date for staff will be sixty (60) or more calendar days from the date that their election form is
approved by human resources.
