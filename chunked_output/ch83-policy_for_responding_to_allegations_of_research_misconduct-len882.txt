the rio will assist the investigation committee in finalizing the draft investigation report, including ensuring
that the respondent's comments are included and considered, and transmit the final investigation report to the
do, who will determine in writing: (1) whether the university accepts the investigation report, its findings, and
the recommended university actions; and (2) the appropriate university actions in response to the accepted
findings of research misconduct. if this determination varies from the findings of the investigation committee,
the do will, as part of his/her written determination, explain in detail the basis for rendering a decision different
from the findings of the investigation committee. alternatively, the do may return the report to the
investigation committee with a request for further fact-finding or analysis.
