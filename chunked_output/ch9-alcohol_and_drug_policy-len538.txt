drug free workplace policy
the university is a drug-free workplace. the university does not tolerate the unlawful manufacture,
dispensing, possession, use, or distribution of illegal drugs, marijuana and/or alcohol by employees
on the university's property, or as part of its activities, except in compliance with this and related
policies. individual departments, due to the nature of their work, may have more stringent policies
concerning prescription drugs.
