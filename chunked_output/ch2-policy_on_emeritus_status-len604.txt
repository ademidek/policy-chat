policy statement:
hrm-1006.1 – definitions
retirement: a person is considered to be retired or a retiree for purposes of this policy if they terminate
employment with the university after meeting all of the university's eligibility requirements for retirement. hrm-1006.2 – policy statement
as outlined in the faculty handbook, certain faculty who retire from the university after providing long and
exceptional service to the university may be eligible for emeritus status. emeritus status may only be granted
by the board of trustees.
