university of richmond
policy manual
purpose:
the purpose of this policy is to ensure that the university of richmond manages the risk associated with legal
agreements and safeguards its assets by providing clear direction to the university community. scope:
this policy applies to the university of richmond, and its affiliates, unless an exemption has been granted by
the executive vice president and chief operating officer, and entities utilizing funds administered by the
university. the policy applies to all binding contractual commitments made by or on behalf of the university of
richmond and/or its affiliates.
