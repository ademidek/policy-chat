policy statement:
acd-8101.1 – definitions
accessioning – the formal process used to accept legally and to record a specimen or object as part of a
collection; the act of accepting objects into the category of materials that a museum holds in the public trust;
the creation of an immediate, brief, and permanent record utilizing a control number for an object or group of
objects added to the collection from the same source at the same time, for which the museum has custody,
right or title.
