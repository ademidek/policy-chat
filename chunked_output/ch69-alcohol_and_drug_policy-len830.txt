 other drugs
• possession
o schedule i or ii drugs – felony- imprisonment from 1 to 10 years or jail for
up to 12 months and a fine of up to $2,500. o schedule iii drugs – misdemeanor – jail for up to 12 months and a fine up to $2500
o schedule iv drugs – misdemeanor – jail for up to 6 months and a fine up to $1000
o schedule v drugs – misdemeanor – fine up to $500
o schedule vi drugs – misdemeanor – fine up to $250
• sales/distribution – schedule i or ii drugs
o felony – imprisonment from 5 to 40 years and a fine up to $100,000
• sales/distribution – schedule iii – v drugs
o misdemeanor – fine up to $250
federal laws governing alcohol & drugs
federal law prohibits the possession, use, or distribution of illegal drugs. there are strict penalties
for drug convictions, including mandatory prison terms for many offenses.
