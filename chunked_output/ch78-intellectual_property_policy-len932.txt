the executive director of foundation, corporate and government relations shall work with the vice
president for business and finance in order to administer recommendations of the intellectual property
committee. the intellectual property committee is responsible for periodic reviews of the intellectual property policy
and consultation regarding intellectual property matters at the university of richmond. the intellectual property committee shall publish such additional interpretations, regulations and
requirements, and shall take such other administrative actions, as are necessary to the suitable discharge of
its duties and the adequate functioning of this policy, including specific provisions for the further
appointment of its members; but in every case the committee's interpretations, regulations and
requirements, as well as its administrative actions, shall be consistent with the provisions expressed in this
policy.
