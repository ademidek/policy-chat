university of richmond | 3
sec-2011.4 – emergency evacuation
international education, in conjunction with ur risk management, will work with ur's travel assistance
provider and other resources to coordinate emergency evacuation if this becomes necessary. if the us
department of state issues a mandatory evacuation order, students in the country must contact the nearest
us embassy or consulate to obtain relevant information and guidance, and must leave the country by the
most expeditious and secure means available. students are also required to contact international education
at the earliest opportunity with information about their whereabouts and plans.
