 safe reporting of overdoses
• "overdose" means a life-threatening condition resulting from the consumption or
use of a controlled substance, alcohol, or any combination of substances. • provides: affirmative defense to prosecution of an individual for the unlawful
purchase, possession, or consumption of alcohol, public intoxication, or possession
of marijuana, a controlled substance or controlled paraphernalia if:
o in good faith, you seek emergency medical attention for yourself or another by reporting
the overdose to 911, law enforcement or medical personnel
