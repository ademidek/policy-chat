prm-1007 – outdoor grill use policy
university of richmond | 2
prm-1007.2 – policy
university-owned grills are provided and permitted for use only at the following designated locations. all
other locations (including the university forum) are prohibited from grill use with the exception of grill use in
tailgating events (see prm-1008 – university tailgating policy). • intramural field picnic area – charcoal grill (reservable)
• the cave: student organization lodge – propane grill (reservable)
• fraternity lodge area – propane grills (for chapter use, non-reservable)
• residential areas near university forest apartments (for individual use, non-reservable)
with the exception of tailgating locations, individuals may not bring or utilize any type of grill on campus
except those provided by the university in designated locations.
