hrm-6002 – compliance training policy
university of richmond | 2
hrm-6002.2 – policy
all university employees, are required to complete compliance and other training as assigned, which will
differ in type and frequency based on their role and responsibilities. specific training requirements are
determined based on an employee's job function and in accordance with other university policies and state
and federal laws and regulations.
hrm-6002.3 - non-compliance with training obligations
failure to complete required training may result in disciplinary actions pursuant to applicable university
polices and procedures.
