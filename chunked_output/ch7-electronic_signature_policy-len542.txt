electronic signature or e-signature — an electronic sound, symbol or process, attached to or logically
associated with an electronic record and executed or adopted by a person with the intent to sign such record. electronic transaction or e-transaction — an action or set of actions that is conducted or performed, in
whole or in part, by electronic means or via electronic records. electronic signature method — the method by which the identity of the applicable signatory and his/her
intent to use an electronic signature has been verified.
