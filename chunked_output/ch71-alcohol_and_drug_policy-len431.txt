forfeiture of personal property and real estate 21 u.s.c. 853
any person convicted of a federal drug offense punishable by more than one year in prison
shall forfeit to the united states any personal or real property related to the violation,
including houses, cars, and other personal belongings. a warrant of seizure is issued and
property is seized at the time an individual is arrested on charges that may result in
forfeiture.
