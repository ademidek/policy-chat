university of richmond | 2
policy statement:
sec-2003.1 – program organization
the university of richmond established the office of emergency management to administer the emergency
management program. the office of emergency management is the central coordinating office for activities
and programs relating to emergency and disaster preparedness, response, recovery, and mitigation among
departments and officials of the university and with similar departments and officials of other universities,
state, and federal agencies; and with other private and quasi-official organizations. the emergency management program organization shall consist of the following:
a) an office of emergency management led by a director.
