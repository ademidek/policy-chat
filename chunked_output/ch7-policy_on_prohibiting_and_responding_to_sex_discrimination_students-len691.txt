gov-3108.1 – policy
the university of richmond prohibits discrimination against applicants, students, faculty, or staff on the
basis of race, color, religion, national or ethnic origin, ethnicity, age, sex, gender, sexual orientation,
gender identity, gender expression, disability, military or veteran status, or any classification protected by
local, state, or federal law.
