charge and composition of the classroom committee
charge:
the classroom committee provides oversight and stewardship of on-campus shared teaching spaces. it oversees
a systematic approach to evaluation, planning and coordinating classroom spaces throughout campus to provide
faculty and students with the best possible learning spaces to support the mission of the university, making
decisions regarding classroom renovations based on curricular needs, student impact, available budget, and
available facilities and is capacity. the committee works to
• serve as good stewards of existing instructional space
• respond to emerging instructional trends and needs of faculty
• gather and prioritize request for improvements to on-campus shared teaching spaces
• create flexible environments to accommodate different pedagogical methods
• monitor and report classroom usage.
