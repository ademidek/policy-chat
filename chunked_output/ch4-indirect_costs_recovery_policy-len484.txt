fin-1201 – indirect costs recovery policy
university of richmond | 2
fin-1201.2 – policy
facilities and administrative (f&a), or indirect, costs are awarded to reimburse the university for
expenses incurred in support of sponsored research and training activities. it is the university's policy to
recover the full amount of indirect costs allowable by a granting agency, whether public or private. the university periodically negotiates a federal f&a reimbursement rate with the u.s.
