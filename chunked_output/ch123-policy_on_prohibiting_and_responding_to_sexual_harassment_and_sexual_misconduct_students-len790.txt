the sexual misconduct review
subcommittee is a subcommittee of the university's threat assessment team. the membership of the sexual misconduct review subcommittee includes:
• the title ix coordinator;
• the chief or assistant chief of the university police department (or a designee who shall be
sworn police officer of the university police department);
• the vice president for student affairs (or a designee who shall be a member of the student
development division); and
• such other members of the university's threat assessment team as the title ix coordinator
deems appropriate. the sexual misconduct review subcommittee will convene within 72 hours of receiving the report of
an incident from the title ix coordinator to review the information provided by the title ix
coordinator.
