higher education information security council (heisc) – supports higher education institutions as they
improve information security governance, compliance, data protection, and privacy programs. information security program – the administrative, technical, or managerial controls used to protect the
confidentiality, integrity, and availability of sensitive or protected information that is stored, processed, or
transmitted by the university and its affiliates. information security standard – specifies requirements for compliance with university information
security and technology policies, other university policies, as well as applicable laws and regulations.
