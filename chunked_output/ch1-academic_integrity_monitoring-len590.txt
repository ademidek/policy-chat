university of richmond
policy manual
purpose:
the purpose of this policy is to ensure regular and rigorous monitoring to support academic integrity,
particularly with respect to the university's student-athletes. scope:
this policy applies to all degree-seeking undergraduate students and credit-bearing undergraduate courses
offered in arts & sciences, the robins school of business, and the jepson school of leadership studies at the
university of richmond. all students, faculty, and staff at the university of richmond are expected to
comply with this policy.
