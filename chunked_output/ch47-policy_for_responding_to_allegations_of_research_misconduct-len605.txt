d. appointment of the inquiry committee
the rio, in consultation with other university officials as appropriate, will appoint an inquiry committee and
committee chair as soon after the initiation of the inquiry as is practical. the inquiry committee must consist of
individuals who do not have unresolved personal, professional, or financial conflicts of interest with those
involved with the inquiry and should include individuals with the appropriate scientific expertise to evaluate the
evidence and issues related to the allegation, interview the principals and key witnesses, and conduct the
inquiry.
