the virginia anti-violence project offers support for
lesbian, gay, bisexual, transgender, queer, and questioning survivors of sexual assault and/or
intimate partner violence as well as hate-motivated violence. virginia's 24/7 lgbtq+
helpline can be reached by calling 1-866-356-6998. this resource is available to students
and employees.
