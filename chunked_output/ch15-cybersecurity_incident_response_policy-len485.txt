irm-4002 – cybersecurity incident response policy
university of richmond | 4
cybersecurity support team – a subgroup of the crisis and emergency management team who will develop
the high-level response, coordination, and decision-making structure during an incident. ur-cirt – composition of the ur-cirt is defined in the ir plan and members are appointed by the vice
president of information services. the team will conduct risk assessment and response in accordance with the
ir plan.
