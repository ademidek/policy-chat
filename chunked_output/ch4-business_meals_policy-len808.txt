policy statement:
4204.1 – definitions
accountable plan
an accountable plan is a reimbursement arrangement or method for reimbursing employees for business
expenses that complies with irs regulations and the related treasury regulations. the accountable plan
requirements must be satisfied to ensure that employees may be reimbursed on a tax-free basis. allowable expense
a necessary, reasonable, and appropriate expense incurred for the primary benefit of the university
appropriate
appropriate means an expense is suitable or fitting in the eyes of an objective third party, and fulfills a valid
business purpose.
