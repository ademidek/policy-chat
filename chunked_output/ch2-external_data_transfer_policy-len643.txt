scope:
this policy applies to the data transfer of restricted or confidential data to an external party performed on a
manual, ad-hoc, or one-off basis. data transfers that occur on an ongoing basis are subject to separate policies
and procedures per the data security policy. index:

policy statement:
irm-4005.1 – definitions
confidential information – sensitive information that must be safeguarded in order to protect the privacy of
individuals and the security and integrity of systems and to guard against fraud.
