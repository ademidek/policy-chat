acd-1605 – policy for creating, modifying, suspending or eliminating
academic programs
university of richmond | 2
policy statement:
acd-1605.1 – definitions
academic programs include degrees (including an undergraduate or graduate certificate) as well as majors,
minors, or concentrations (if the concentration is recorded on a student's transcript). degree: a degree is the certification that is awarded by the university upon completion of a specific course
of study. for example, c.a.s (undergraduate certificate), g.c.
