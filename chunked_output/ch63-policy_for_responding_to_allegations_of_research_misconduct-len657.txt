documentation of decision not to investigate
if the do decides that an investigation is not warranted, the rio shall secure and maintain for 7 years
after the termination of the inquiry sufficiently detailed documentation of the inquiry to permit a later
assessment by ori of the reasons why an investigation was not conducted. these documents must be
provided to ori or other authorized hhs personnel upon request. in situations where an investigation
is not warranted, the do and the rio are free to follow up to ensure that best research practices are
followed at the university.
