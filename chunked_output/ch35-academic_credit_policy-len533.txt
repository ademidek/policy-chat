credit for clinics
credit for clinics is based on the total time the student spends engaging in approved clinic-
associated activities. these activities may include time spent in a regularly scheduled
classroom component of the clinic; time spent preparing assignments for the clinic class; or
time spent preparing for, or engaging in, clinic-associated client representation. students
are required to track the total time they spend engaged in approved clinic-associated
activities and verify they have met the required total hours.
