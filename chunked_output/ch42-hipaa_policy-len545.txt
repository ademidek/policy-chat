hrm-1005 – hipaa privacy policy and procedures
university of richmond | 7
hrm-1005.5- maintaining appropriate documentation regarding compliance with hipaa
a. the university will maintain documentation, in written or electronic form, of policies, procedures,
communications, and other administrative documents as required by 45 cfr § 164.530(i) and (j) for a
period of at least six (6) years from the date of creation or the date when last in effect, whichever is later. this policy will be kept in the university security documentation library.
