university of richmond
policy manual
purpose:
the purpose of this policy is to outline the roles and responsibilities for establishing and maintaining the
university's emergency management program. scope:
this policy addresses the university-wide emergency management program and applies to the entire
university community, specifically those with responsibilities identified in the university crisis and
emergency management plan, recovery plan, and other university related emergency management plans and
procedures. the office of emergency management shall perform emergency management functions within
the territorial limits of the university including those properties operated but not owned by the university.
