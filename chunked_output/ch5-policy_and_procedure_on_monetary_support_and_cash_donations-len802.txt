permissible use of university resources
while the university does not, in general, provide monetary assistance to other non-profit organizations, the
university is committed to being an engaged member of the greater richmond community and partner with
organizations across the metro area and within the academy. permissible use of university resources
include:
• the attendance by one or two students, staff, faculty, or administrators of the university at events
where there is a clear business purpose for the attendance. the cost of attendance at such events
may be paid from university budgets allocated for business meals and travel in accordance with
applicable policies;
• marketing at events or in publications or other venues in which part of the fee could be considered a
charitable donation.
