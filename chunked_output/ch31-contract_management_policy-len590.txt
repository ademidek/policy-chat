fin-4408 – contract management policy
university of richmond | 6
fin-4408.8 – roles and responsibilities
all individuals responsible for the purchase of goods and services on the university's behalf must be familiar
with contracting requirements and this policy. responsibility of contract requestor
contract requestors are responsible for inputting the contract record into the cms. responsibility of the contract manager
contract managers are identified by their department head and shall be adequately skilled in contract
management according to the level and complexity of the contract.
