university of richmond
policy manual
purpose:
the university of richmond ("richmond") is committed to safeguarding the privacy of personal data. this
policy is in response to regulation (eu) 2016/679 (commonly known as the general data protection
regulation or the gdpr), and provides individuals located in the european union ("eu"), and in the european
economic area (eea) with the required notice of how richmond collects, uses, and disclosures personal
information provided to richmond. s
cope:
this policy applies to all individuals located in the european union (eu) and in the european economic area
(eea), who submit personal information and/or use richmond's websites and other services.
