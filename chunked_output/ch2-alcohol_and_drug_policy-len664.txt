scope:
the alcohol, tobacco and other drug policy applies to all students, staff, and faculty as well as third party
users of university facilities. this policy applies to conduct that occurs on the campus of the university, on or
in off-campus buildings or property of the university and at university sponsored activities, including off-
campus education programs and activities and public property adjacent to the university. t
his policy also applies to university students studying abroad through a university approved study abroad
program.
