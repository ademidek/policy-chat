data classification: details and examples of university data classification levels are contained in the
university's data security policy. university data is classified as: confidential, restricted, official use only,
or public. data stewardship domain: a university administrative or operational area, function or process and the
authoritative data elements created, collected, and managed by that area throughout the lifecycle of that data,
regardless of the transactional system(s) in which they reside (e.g., finance data).
